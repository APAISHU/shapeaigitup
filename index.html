Sudhamurthy

Sudha Murty (born 19 August 1950) is a Padma Shri awardee, Indian Engineering teacher, Kannada, Marathi and English author as well as a Social worker. She is also the Chairperson of the Infosys Foundation. She is married to the co-founder of Infosys, N. R. Narayana Murthy. She is a member of the public health care initiatives of the Gates Foundation.
